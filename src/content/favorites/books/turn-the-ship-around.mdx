The best book I read about leadership. It's a must-read for anyone who wants to lead a team, regardless of the size of the team or the industry. The book tells the story of how Captain David Marquet transformed the worst submarine in the US Navy into the best one. The book is full of practical examples and tips on how to lead a team in a way that empowers everyone to make decisions and take responsibility. It's a great book for anyone who wants to improve their leadership skills.
